ERP for Healthcare

ERP in Healthcare is positioned for accelerated growth among hospitals through enhanced operational efficiency & reduced operational costs.

4C Solutions provides innovative and cost effective ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) services for the health care organizations like Hospitals & Diagnostic Laboratories, which seeks to maximise efficiency and productivity. The real-time data will help in better patient management, patient billing, diagnostic laboratory management etc. We intend our customers to concentrate on their core competency: Providing patients with the right care at the right time. Our comprehensive portfolio of solutions creates significant value for Health care organizations.

OPD Management

Patient Registration, Appointment Booking, Consultation, Vital Signs, Complaints & Diagnosis, Fee Validity

IPD Management

Patient Billing, Patient ADT(Admission, Transfer, Discharge), Clinical Procedure, Discharge Summary, Wards Occupancy Status, Vital Signs, IPD Consultation

Hospital/Clinic Management

Staff, Medical Departments, Healthcare Service Units (Wards & Beds, ICU, OT etc.), Healthcare Service Unit Charges

Patient Management

Patient Details, Medical History, Risk Factors, Patient's Relations with existing patients

Healthcare Practitioner

Practitioner Details, Practitioner Schedule & Availability, Practitioner Charges, Referring Physicians

Medical Coding Standards

We support medical coding standards like International Classification of Diseases (ICD-10) to code & classify Diseases, Signs & Symptoms

Pharmacy

Supplier Management, Inventory Management, Batch Number & Expiry Dates, Point of Sales, Multiple POS Profiles, GST Compliance

Laboratory Management

Managed Sample Collection, Lab Tests, Lab Reports, Antibiotic & Sensitivity, SMS & Email Notifications

Core Modules

Accounts, Stock, Human Resources, Employee Payroll, Asset Management, Vendor Management, CRM & many more
