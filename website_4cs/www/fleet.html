Fleet Management

Technological adoption for vehicle tracking is a must for businesses that rely on transport.

4C Solutions provides innovative and cost effective GPS Tracking System (GTS) for real-time geo-location of your fleet. It provides native android mobile application for tracking your fleet on the go to enhance efficiency and productivity. Our Fleet management solution creates significant value for any organisation that relies on transport by providing multiple kinds of alerts and reports.

Note: Some features are dependent on Sensors & GPS Device Capabilities.

Vehicle Tracking

Live Location, Location History, Remote Power Cut-Off, Total Distance, Maximum Speed, Average Speed, Engine Hours

Events and Notifications

Mobile, SMS and Email Notifications for Alarms, Vehicle Motion, Ignition On/Off, Speed Limit Exceeded, Maintenance Alerts, Vehicle Status (Online/ Offline/ Unknown)

Reports

Trip Reports, Summary Reports, Event Reports, Activity/Route Report, Stops Report

Driver Behaviour

Driver & Vehicle Association, Hard Braking, Hard Acceleration, Hard Cornering, High RPM, Lane Change, Over speeding

Alarms

SOS, Vibration, Movement, Lowspeed, Overspeed, Geo-Fence Enter/Exit, Accident, Tow, Idle, Low Battery, Power Cut & Restored, Door Status

Vehicle Maintenance

Maintenance alerts based on Total Distance, Engine Hours
